Lay-up tray - Catering

Small oval plate (bread plate)
Large oval plate with 2 multi-purpose dishes

  • 1 butter dish
  • 1 empty (to hold Extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar)
Cutlery
  • Butter knife
  • Fork
  • Knife
  • Dessert spoon
Western/feature starter (e.g. smoked salmon):
  • On a medium plate with a plastic cover
  • A food presentation ring may be in place around starter

Lay-up tray – Crew

Remove Starter
Add:

  • Base plate
  • Folded linen napkin
  • Extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper grinders at centre of large oval plate
  • Tumbler
  • Prepared bread presentation bowl
  • Champagne flute/wine glass if applicable
Check the lay-up tray items are correct and clean.

Suite 1A/F
Table Preparation

Place items on table in the sequence of furthest to closest:

  1. Linen

Table outline:

  1. Bread plate
  2. Bread presentation bowl
  3. Extra virgin olive oil
  4. Balsamic Vinegar
  5. Large oval plate
  6. Champagne flute / Wine glass
  7. Tumbler glass

Cutlery:

  1. Butter knife
  2. Fork
  3. Knife
  4. Dessert spoon

  5. Base plate
  6. Linen napkin

Verbally highlight:
Speciality Bread i.e.

  • Garlic Bread (if requested)
  • Melba toast (served with caviar)
  • Arabic & Iranian Bread and Markook Bread (Arabic Mezze)
Dipping Bowl for Extra virgin olive oil and Balsamic Vinegar

Suite 1A/F
Table Clearance

Clear items on table in the sequence of closest to furthest:

P – Plate (and accompanying plates)
A – Accompaniment (condiment etc.)
C – Cutlery (remaining on table)
C – Closest to furthest
  1. Plate (including Base Plate and cutlery on the plate)
  2. Accompaniment (i.e. condiment in multi-purpose dish)

Cutlery:

  1. Remaining cutlery on the table from closest to furthest

Table outline:

  1. Tumbler glass
  2. Champagne flute / Wine glass
  3. Large oval plate
  4. Balsamic Vinegar
  5. Extra virgin olive oil
  6. Bread presentation bowl
  7. Bread plate

  8. Linen

  • Fold the linen corners into the centre of the table
  • Start with the corners nearest customer
  • Make 1 more fold in the middle
  • Hold the linen under the silver tray

After delivery of the hot towel, offer a mattress to enhance the customers comfort. Inform the customer that you will be monitoring the cabin throughout the flight should they require further assistance.

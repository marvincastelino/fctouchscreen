Customer Care in First Class

We Care

Category 3, 4 and 5 Flights
We Care involves a cycle of 3 duties

One crew member is required to complete one cycle of duties. The duration of one cycle should be 30 minutes


Customer Care.

  • Carry a small lined silver tray
  • Be considerate e.g. put a blanket/duvet over a customer who is asleep, switch reading light on/off as appropriate
  • Promote the Duty Free service

Cleanliness – Cabin & Lavatories.

  • Carry a cabin tidy bag
  • Use a dust pan and brush if needed
  • Lightly spray air freshener if required
  • Replenish lavatory products (not only CSA’s duty)
  • Regularly check lavatory cleanliness (origami)

Refreshments.

  • Take a drink order
  • Ask if customer would like to order food, do not wait for the customer to initiate a call
